The Formula of BMI

Q: What is the formula of BMI(Body Mass Index)?
A: BMI = w/h2 Weight(kg)/Height(m)2

FAQ about BMI

Q: Why do bodybuilders have high BMI?
A: The muscles are more dense than fat. Sometimes bodybuilders may be considered overweight because of their highly-trained muscles. Those people might have high BMI, but they don't have to lose weight as long as they have low body fat percentage.

Q: Is my waist circumference considered healthy？
A: Waist Circumference of Male over 90 cm and Waist Circumference of Female over 80 cm are considered unhealthy.
