Number Guessing

The classic childhood game

Instructions:

Choose two numbers as the range. Press the 'Generate a Number' button to randomly generate a number. The system will automatically pick a number within the range.

Due to the fairness, our system would not display the number on the screen.

Then, enter your answer down below, and press the 'confirm' button.

Once the player get the right number, the game ends.

(Players will have to pick a number in range. If players guess the wrong number, the game continues.)
