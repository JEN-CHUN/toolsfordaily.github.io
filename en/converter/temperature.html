The Formula of Temperature Conversion

Celsius
Fahrenheit to Celsius

°C = (°F − 32) * 5/9

Kelvin to Celsius

°C = K − 273.15

Rankine to Celsius

°C = (R − 491.67) * 5/9

Fahrenheit
Celsius to Fahrenheit

°F = 9/5(°C + 32)

Kelvin to Fahrenheit

°F = K * 9/5 − 459.67

Rankine to Fahrenheit

°F = R − 459.67

Rankine
Celsius to Rankine

R = (°C + 273.15) * 9/5

Fahrenheit to Rankine

R = °F + 459.67

Kelvin to Rankine

R = K * 9/5

Kelvin
Celsius to Kelvin

K = °C + 273.15

Fahrenheit to Kelvin

K = (°F + 459.67) * 5/9

Rankine to Kelvin

K = R * 5/9

FAQ of the Temperature Units

Q: What is 0 degree Celsius in Fahrenheit?
A: 0 degree Celsius 32 degree Fahrenheit

Q: What is 100 degree Celsius in Fahrenheit?
A: 100 degree Celsius = 212 degree Fahrenheit

Q: What is 100 degree Fahrenheit in Celsius?
A: 100 degree Fahrenheit ≈ 37.8 degree Celsius

Q: What is 0 degree Fahrenheit in Fahrenheit?
A: 0 degree Fahrenheit ≈ -17.8 degree Celsius
